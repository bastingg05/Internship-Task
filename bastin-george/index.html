About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour online hackathon designed to unite developers, designers, and innovators to build impactful solutions.

Whether you're an experienced hacker or just getting started, this is your chance to collaborate, create, and compete with the brightest minds from around the world.

FAQs

Is it free to join?

Yes! HackSphere 2025 is completely free and open to all participants globally.

Can I join solo?

Yes, solo participation is welcome! You can also form a team of up to 4 members.

What can I build?

You can build web apps, mobile apps, tools, AI projects, and anything innovative. Surprise us!
